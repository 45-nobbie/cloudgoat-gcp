# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be professional, be kind, and don't be a jerk.

## Our standards
- Treat others with respect and courtesy.
- Be welcoming to newcomers; assumptions about skill or background are pointless.
- Do not harass, shame, or target individuals or groups.

## Enforcement
If you observe or are subject to unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`. Reports will be handled confidentially.
